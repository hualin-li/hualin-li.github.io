Research Interest

I'm a decision theorist and mathematical economist who pursues the axiomatic and operational studies of the following areas.

  • Decision Theory;   Welfare Economics;   Game Theory;   Social Choice.<\li>
  • (Hence, D0, D6, D7, and D8 categories in JEL classification.)
In particular, I am interested in the axiomatization of all kind. Currently, my research focuses on the axiomatic fundations of (i) decision-making under explicit and variable information; (ii) leading decision.
